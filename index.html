What is an MRI?

MRI, or Magnetic Resonance Imaging, is a painless, non-invasive exam that can produce images of the entire body without harmful radiation like x-rays. It is used to facilitate precise diagnosis and treatment as well as assessments prior to surgery or other medical procedures.
It is the most advanced form of medical imaging technology available. Several Nobel Peace Prizes have been awarded for advancements discovered during the development of MRI technology!
